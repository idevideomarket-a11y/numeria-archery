# OUTPUT 6 - NUMERIA WORLD SHELL

## 1. MAGICAL ACADEMY FRAMING

### Academy of Numeria Concept
The game is framed as enrollment in the **Academy of Numeria**, a prestigious magical school where young archers learn the ancient art of "Numerical Archery" - shooting arrows that solve mathematical mysteries.

**Narrative Hook:**
- Player is a new student at the Academy
- Each world represents a different "training ground" or "magical realm" for practice
- Progression = advancing through Academy ranks (Novice → Apprentice → Scholar → Master)
- Stars earned = "Merit Badges" that unlock new Academy areas

**Academy Visual Elements:**
- Academy crest/logo appears on loading screens
- Diploma-style level completion certificates
- Report card summary showing progress
- Graduation ceremony animation at Level 100

A bright, welcoming beginner's field where new Academy students take their first shots. Gentle rolling hills, wildflowers, and friendly butterflies create a safe, encouraging environment.

## 5. LOCKED/UNLOCKED VISUAL LOGIC

### Locked World State
**Visual Treatment:**
- Grayscale or desaturated
- Lock icon overlay
- Fog/cloud cover
- "?" mystery indicator

**Interaction:**
- Tap shows unlock requirements
- Numi explains what's needed
- Preview button (grayed out)
- "Keep playing to unlock!" message

**Locked Preview:**
- Silhouette of world visible
- World name hidden or "???"
- Star requirement displayed
- Progress bar toward unlock

### Unlocked World State
**Visual Treatment:**
- Full, vibrant colors
- No overlay
- Clear, bright presentation
- Animated elements

**Interaction:**
- Tap to enter world
- Play button prominent
- Star collection visible
- Progress within world shown

### Unlock Animation Sequence
```
1. Player earns final needed star
2. Screen flashes with golden light
3. Camera pans to locked world on map
4. Lock shatters with particle effect
5. Fog clears revealing full color
6. World name appears with fanfare
7. Numi celebrates with special animation
8. "New World Unlocked!" banner
9. Option to visit immediately or continue
```

# OUTPUT 7 - QUINN CHARACTER SYSTEM

## 1. QUINN - MAIN PLAYABLE CHARACTER

### Character Overview
**Quinn** is the player's avatar - a cheerful, determined young archer-in-training at the Academy of Numeria. Quinn represents the player in the game world and serves as their mathematical journey companion.

**Character Traits:**
- Age: 7-8 years old (relatable to players)
- Personality: Curious, brave, encouraging, persistent
- Expression: Always friendly and approachable
- Style: Academy uniform with personal customization
